Welcome to UBigkas

UBigkas is an interactive platform designed to help students learn and master Filipino grammar. With quizzes, grammar correction tools, and detailed explanations, students can enhance their understanding of sentence structures, parts of speech, and correct usage in real-life writing and communication.

Interactive Quizzes

Test your knowledge with sentence structure quizzes, verb conjugations, pronouns, adjectives, and more!

Grammar Correction

Automatically check and correct your Filipino sentences with our AI-powered grammar tool.

Detailed Explanations

Learn why a sentence is correct or incorrect with step-by-step feedback and rule-based guidance.
