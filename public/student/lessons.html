Filipino Grammar Rules

I-hover ang card para makita ang kahulugan.

1. Pangngalan (Noun)

A word used to name a person, place, thing, or idea.

2. Pandiwa (Verb)

A word that expresses an action, occurrence, or state of being.

3. Pang-uri (Adjective)

A word that describes or modifies a noun or pronoun.

4. Pang-abay (Adverb)

Modifies a verb, adjective, or adverb, indicating manner, time, or place.

5. Panghalip (Pronoun)

A word that replaces a noun to avoid repetition (e.g., siya, ito, kami).
